Interaction notes

§04 · the tactile details worth preserving in the native port

Chat typing rhythm

Mia's bubbles appear with a 700–1400ms "typing" dot cluster before each message. Stagger is uneven — feels human, not scripted.

Plan generation spinner

"Mia is cooking up your week…" micro-copy rotates through 4 phrases (~3s each) while the LLM streams. Copy variants in plan-screen.jsx.

Day switcher

Day pills (Mon–Sun) have a spring on tap — selected day scales 1.08x for 180ms then settles. Meals cross-fade (opacity 120ms).

Grocery row check

Tapping a row checks it and strikethroughs. Loyalty pills (PC $0.75) pulse briefly on first load to draw attention.

Sheet presentation

Modals ("Send to store", "Swap meal") use a 22px-radius bottom sheet with iOS-standard detents (medium, large). Backdrop at 40% ink.

Check-in sliders

Weight slider snaps to 0.2lb; haptic .light on each step. Energy/adherence 1–5 buttons are segmented pills — selected state uses olive fill.
